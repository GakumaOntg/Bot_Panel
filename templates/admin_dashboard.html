How to Control the Bot

This panel only monitors the bot. To start, stop, or switch bots, you must go to the Render.com dashboard.

  1. Go to the my-bot-runner service.
  2. Click the Environment tab.
  3. Change the value of BOT_TO_RUN to one of the bot names listed below (e.g., client_b).
  4. Click "Save Changes" to deploy the new bot.
